About Us

Check out the most exclusive, intimate venue in Lake County.

Host your next event at Petite Soirée and bring your vision to life.

Our open floor plan, all white canvas, 1400 SQ Ft modern event space, makes Petite Soirée the perfect venue to celebrate life‘s best moments.

This location provides a one of a kind ambiance for your chic, upscale event with your closest friends and family members.

Birthday Parties (Sweet 16 and up) Bridal Showers Baby Showers Gender Reveals Corporate events Networking events Holiday Parties Classes and workshops Pop Up Shops Media Shoots (Video/Photo) Seminars and more! Check out our affordable venue packages.

Rental Policy

Standard Events: Booking Duration

Venue Packages are booked at a minimum of 6 hours: 1-hour set up 4-hour event 1-hour breakdown

Rental Policy

Confirm your booking
1. Review and sign our rental agreement 2. Submit a 50% deposit to secure your date. 3. The remaining balance will be due two weeks before your event. The remaining balance also includes a refundable security deposit of $200, which is refunded within 2 days after your event. Last minute bookings (events booked two weeks before the date of event) must be paid in full upon booking.
Schedule Appointment

Smoking Policy

With the exception of Hookah, smoking is permitted.

Cancellation/COVID-19 Policy

All deposits are non-refundable. However, management will diligently work to secure a future date for your event within a 12-month grace period, from the date of cancellation. This courtesy is not transferable to another individual or organization.
